How to add a song

Open worship.html and duplicate any <li class="song-card"> block. Fill in the title, artist, optional theme note, and two links: a Listen link (the song on Spotify) and a Lyrics link (to a legal source such as Genius, Hymnary, or the artist's official lyrics page).

Please do not paste lyric text directly into the page. Link out instead. This keeps Room 38 in good standing with songwriters and publishers.

To swap the embedded Spotify playlist, copy the new playlist ID from its share URL (the long alphanumeric string after /playlist/) and replace it in both the iframe src on this page and the homepage worship section in index.html.
